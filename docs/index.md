# GED Science Prep

Welcome to the GED Science preparation resource! This comprehensive curriculum covers all 200 assessment targets required for the GED Science Test.

## About This Course

This course is designed for adult learners preparing for the GED Science Test. It covers:

- **200 Concepts** organized into 12 chapters
- **8 Science Practices** that reflect how scientists work
- **3 Content Domains**: Life Science, Physical Science, and Earth and Space Science
- **Interactive Learning** through MicroSims and visualizations

## Course Structure

The curriculum is organized into 12 chapters:

1. Scientific Reasoning Foundations
2. Comprehending Scientific Materials
3. Designing and Conducting Scientific Investigations
4. Reasoning from Data and Evidence
5. Statistical Methods and Sampling
6. Descriptive Statistics and Probability
7. Evaluating Scientific Claims and Theories
8. Scientific Models and Theories
9. Expressing and Communicating Scientific Information
10. Life Science: Human Body and Health
11. Life Science: Energy Flow and Cellular Organization
12. Life Science: Genetics, Heredity, and Evolution
